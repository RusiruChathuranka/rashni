Senior Software Engineers - ReactJS / React Native

About INTAAP

INTAAP is a company that specializes in providing mobile and website solutions to its clients. They have a team of experienced professionals who work together to deliver tech solutions. They offer a wide range of services including strategy, production, design, development, and support for mobile and website projects. They work with clients across various industries and are dedicated to delivering high-quality work through collaboration and a high level of service. The company focuses on creating innovative and effective solutions to help their clients reach their business goals.

Job description

We are seeking top-tier talent to join our team of engineers, product managers, and designers at our company. We are looking for individuals who are passionate about creating software that makes a positive impact on the world, possess a strong sense of initiative, and have a team-first mentality. As a small, lean team, your role will have a direct impact on the success of the company.

As a Full Stack Engineer, you will be :
  • Responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining JavaScript-based, React and Node.js web and mobile applications
  • Implementing and updating back-end and front-end components, following best coding practices, and working with cross-functional teams to ship software on-time and on-quality.
To be considered for the role:
  • You should have solid computer science fundamentals, excellent problem-solving skills, expert-level proficiency in JavaScript, React, and Node.js
  • Strong written & verbal. communication skills
  • Be familiar with Agile methodology.
  • A Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field is preferred.

We offer an exciting and passionate working environment within a young, fast-growing company, the opportunity to work with a high-performing team, a competitive salary package, and the ability to work remotely (with a stable internet connection).
